15 # File operations
16 
17 ```bash
18 # to rename a file
19 :E # or :Explorer
20 R # rename at bottom
21 :bp # go back previous buffer
22 ```

24 # Copy/Pasting
25 
26 In normal mode:
27 
28 |command|effect|
29 |---|---|
30 |3y, p|copy 3 lines, paste after cursor|
31 |"ayy, "ap|copy current line to buffer a, paste from buffer a|
32 |v/V|start selecting character, whole line|
33 |5ly|yanks into default buffer 5 characters from cursor|
34 |"hy|yanks into register h, use + register for system clipboard|
35 
36 In insert mode:
37 
38 |command|effect|
39 |---|---|
40 |`C-r{register}|paste contents of the register|
41 |C-r"|to paste from default register|
42 |C-u|to delete everything before the cursor on this line|
43 |C-w|to delete from cursor to the beginning of previous word|
44 
45 # Move Around
46 
47 In normal mode:
48 
49 |command|effect|
50 |---|---|
51 |100l|go right 100 positions, useful to navigate a long line.|
52 |10w/10b/10e|go forward/backward 10 words, e end of word|
53 |fa, ta|move to or just before the next letter a|
54 |/pattern|move to the next word matches, n/N to repeat search|
55 |H,M,L # to go to top/mid/bottom of screen|
56 |1G(1gg), G|go to beginning/end of file|
57 |),}|jump forward one sentence/paragraph|
58 |%|jump to matching bracket|
59 
60 
61 # Editing
62 
63 In normal mode:
64 
65 |command|effect|
66 |---|---|
67 |dw/W|delete word before/after cursor|
68 |x,r|delete,replace character at cursor|
69 |.,u,ctrl-r|repeat, undo, redo|
70 |q<letter>, @<letter>|record operations and reapply, type q again to quit|
